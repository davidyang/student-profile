About Me

I grew up in Ames, Iowa, and was a math major at the University of Chicago. Liking life in Central Iowa, I returned to Ames and began graduate studies in Theoretical Condensed Matter Physics. Becoming frustrated with a culture that does not value clear and elegant writing, and not excited by the effort/reward ratio on the work that remains to be done in that field, I started spending more and more time on other interests ... Now I have followed my wife into the New York area, and am excited to be using the most modern tools for software development!

Vision Statements

What experiences do I want to have through coding? Where do I want coding to take me?

I see myself making everything about computers fitting my desires: no annoyances, no discomfort, minimal fatigue. I see myself creating and distributing tools that bring my view of ideal computer interaction to other users.

What will I contribute to the world through coding?

I will produce tools that make people be and feel more in control of their computers and of their data.
